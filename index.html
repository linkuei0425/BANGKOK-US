Airport Guide

Book Premium Transfer

(1) Airport Rail Link (ARL)

  • Location: Basement B1 Level.
  • Duration: ~26-30 mins to Phaya Thai (Terminal Station).
  • Transfers:
    • Makkasan Station (A6): Transfer to MRT Blue Line (Phetchaburi Station).
    • Phaya Thai Station (A8): Transfer to BTS Sukhumvit Line.
  • Hours: 05:30 – 24:00 (Every 10-15 mins).

(2) Public Taxi

  • Location: Level 1, outside Gates 4 and 7. Get a ticket from the kiosk.
  • Cost: Metered fare + 50 THB Airport Surcharge + Toll fees (~75 THB). Total ~350-500 THB.

(3) Ride-Hailing (Grab / Bolt)

  • Location: Dedicated pick-up points (usually Level 1 outside Gates 4 or 7).
  • Pros: Transparent pricing and varied car types.

(4) LimoBus Express

  • Routes: 2 lines heading to Khao San Road and Silom/Siam.
  • Cost: ~150 THB. Direct access to tourist hubs.

(1) SRT Red Line

  • Location: 10-15 mins walk following signs.
  • Route: Airport → Krung Thep Aphiwat Central Terminal (Bang Sue).
  • Cost & Time: ~33 THB, approx 17-20 mins.
  • Transfer: Switch to MRT Blue Line at the Central Terminal.

(2) Airport Bus (A1-A4)

— Best for BTS transfers

  • Location: T1 Exit 6 or T2 Exit 12.
  • Route A1 (30 THB): To BTS Mo Chit / MRT Chatuchak. Every 5 mins (Highly Recommended).
  • Route A2 (30 THB): To Victory Monument.
  • Route A4 (60 THB): To Khao San Road.

(3) Public Taxi

  • Location: Dedicated taxi stand outside Arrivals.
  • Cost: Metered fare + 50 THB Airport Surcharge + Tolls. Total ~250-400 THB.
